About the Podcast

Real conversations
about building right

Banni Mathadona brings together the experts you wish you'd spoken to before breaking ground - structural engineers, construction managers, interior designers, and finance specialists - all talking in the language of real Bangalore homebuilders.

No jargon. No scripts. Just honest, specific, practical knowledge for anyone who's about to build, currently building, or realising they should have asked more questions before building.
